Why Image Compression Matters for SEO

Image compression is a critical factor for website performance and search engine optimization. Large image files can significantly slow down your website, leading to poor user experience and lower search rankings.

Benefits of Image Compression:

  • Faster Page Loading: Compressed images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO Rankings: Google considers page speed as a ranking factor
  • Reduced Bandwidth Usage: Saves hosting bandwidth and costs
  • Improved Mobile Experience: Faster loading on mobile devices
  • Higher Conversion Rates: Faster sites typically have better conversion rates

Best Practices for Image Optimization:

  1. Choose the right format (JPG for photos, PNG for graphics, WebP for modern browsers)
  2. Resize images to the exact dimensions needed on your website
  3. Use appropriate compression level (70-85% for JPG usually maintains quality)
  4. Consider using responsive images with srcset attribute
  5. Implement lazy loading for images below the fold
